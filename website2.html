The requirements for University of Waterloo

As one of the best University for computer science in the world, University of Waterloo's computer science related programs are certainly not easy to get into, here are the requirements for Ontario students: six Grade 12 U and/or M courses including:
  • Advanced Functions
  • Calculus and Vectors
  • Any Grade 12 U English
